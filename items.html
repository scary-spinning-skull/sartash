Items of Interest


There are countless magical items found throughout the land. Some are of human manufacture, some are made by lizardmen, and some are of unknown origin.

Detection Statuette


A small statuette of Christ made of an incredibly light ivory-colored material. There is a WIS+50% chance of the statuette flashing a light and issuing a warning when openly hostile non-Christians are approaching the possessor of the statue. The statuette can also trigger when schismatics are near, hostile or no (50%), or when Satanists or their magical works are nearby (80%).

A small loop along the back of the idol allows the owner to wear it as a necklace. There are three settings: On, Mute, and Off. The statue's light flashes in Mute mode but does not speak, and it becomes inert when changed to Off.
