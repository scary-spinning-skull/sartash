Reputation


When entering a new community or town, roll a 1d10 and add the result to whatever the "default" is for that town or faction. For tribal lizardmen, this number will be 25, while the default is 50 for civilized communities. Christian communities, including communities of converted lizardmen, start at 75.

Reputation and Effects
1-5 Hated. Villages will not let you into their communities and may use spells such as Darkness to keep you away. Accompanying characters who are not themselves Hated will be treated as such so long as you are with them. -3 to attitude roll during encounters. 25% chance of you being followed and harassed with one round of slingstone attacks after setting up camp
5-15 Loathed. Will only be allowed into a community briefly, and under armed guard. 50% surcharge on all goods and a 5gp "entry tax". Accompanying characters who are not Loathed will be treated as such so long as you are with them. -2 to attitude roll during encounters.
16-30 As above, but hearts can be softened with enough groveling and bribes - 10gp per party member to the guards and city leadership will earn you fairer treatment. 5gp entry tax, 25% surcharge on all goods, -1 to attitude rolls during encounters.
31-55 Treated with polite disdain. Party may rest in town for two nights, no longer. Token entry tax demanded, 15% surcharge on all goods unless there is a lizardman in the party - then it is merely 10%. Helpers found in town will demand a "bonus" up front in addition to their regular pay.
56-70 You are "one of the good ones". A token entry tax of a few coppers will be demanded. You may lodge for a week or longer, and there is a modest 5% surcharge on all goods sold to you unless you have a lizardman in the party. +1 to attitude rolls.
71-85 A credit to your race. No entry tax, and lodging can be secured indefinitely (provided that you pay). No surcharge on goods. +2 to attitude rolls.
86-97 A good egg. Townslizards are happy to see you, and several days of free lodging are offered. A retainer, a warrior-in-training, will be offered for one blue month (twelve days) free of charge on the assurance that he will be allowed to experience some combat, and three tribeslizards are willing to accompany you for three days. If the retainer comes home dead, you will lose 5 reputation points in that specific community. +3 to attitude rolls.
98-100 Town hero. As above, but a 5% discount is offered on goods in town. Two retainers will be offered for a blue month instead of one, and you will lose five reputation points in the community if both die in your service.
