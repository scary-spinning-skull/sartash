St. Irene

2000 99% 75 (2,0) 75%
St. Irene is the capital of New Canaan. New arrivals from Earth are brought here and acclimated to life on their new world, and lizardmen, converts and pagans alike, choose to come here because they are able to find community and facilities far more easily than in other areas ruled by humans. The city is the namesake of the Abbey of St. Irene, which sits in the center of the city. Various shrines to saints (St. Dorothea, St. Monica, St. Edwen, St. Ia, and St. Agnes) and the only male monastery in New Canaan, St. Bononio's, is found here as well.

Ruins of a temple dedicated to Karchak. A 30' statue of Karchak with its frill, groin, and chest chiseled off lays on the ground at the base of a stepped pedestal. There are stones scattered around which are heavily weathered, but it is obvious that they were worked once. Graffitti from centuries of lizardman and human travellers litters the area; the most significant are the words "THIS AREA CLEANSED BY SRA. JOAN OF NEW ENGLAND AND SRA. ATTRACTA 1127". There is a small catacomb and ritual space which was once sealed with a thick flagstone. A party of raiders from the Gazg tribe established a camp on the site in early August 1300.

What is "% to find"?
This refers to the chance to find whatever the characters are looking for in a particular community. The specific listed percentage refers to weapons that are valued at 4 GP or under in the core rulebook. The players must roll at this number or lower if they want to find an item in the community for this week. This number drops by 5% for every additional 1 GP in value, but may fall further - or rise - depending on player actions in the community or faction, as merchants may find themselves being willing to take one last look in the stockroom etc if the group are people whom they like.
